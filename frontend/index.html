Connecting Students to a Faster Future

PrimeNet Hostel Broadband Management System is custom-designed for educational campus environments. We specialize in managing dense network loads while ensuring each individual room receives high-speed, secure, and low-latency bandwidth.

  • MAC-Authenticated Security: We register your device's MAC address directly to our router database for password-free, secure logins.
  • Room-to-Room Mapping: Enables structured troubleshooting and network health checks across hostel buildings.
  • Student Budget-Friendly: Optimized rate structuring at just ₹30/month (billed semi-annually as ₹180 for 6 months).
  • High Availability: Secondary backup links ensure 99.9% uptime during crucial exam and class times.
